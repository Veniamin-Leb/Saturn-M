About us History

The Company's mission is to maximize customer satisfaction, supplying reliable and highly efficient gearboxes and drive systems, providing a network of high-quality and a qualified service

Since 2008 we have been developing special gears and drive systems to meet the technical challenges of today and tomorrow. The gear manufacturing process of the cemented gears with gear finishing operation, which was introduced into our production in 2010, is considered worldwide as a benchmark for the highest level of precision and quality of production. Our modern high-precision equipment and human resources are in line with current technical requirements and trends.

To be closer to the customer and to optimize our delivery times for products and spare parts, in 2020 we expanded our capabilities through international alliances in India, Turkey and Italy.

Conditions

What are the deadlines?

Terms are determined individually in accordance with the volume of the order and the place where the delivery will be carried out.

How is the delivery carried out?

The price and delivery time depends on the country and city to which you need to deliver the equipment, to clarify the questions of interest, write to us by mail, it is located on the contact page.

What is the order of interaction?

We offer engineering solutions taking into account the specifics of the tasks, including the replacement of individual drives and other mechanisms before the complex replacement of all equipment. Our regional representatives are able to assess in detail the tasks assigned to us without delay and offer their optimal solutions. Based on extensive experience, we can usually offer a job option that improves the performance or extends the period of work.

How can I contact you?

You can contact us by going to the "Contacts" page, there will be an up-to-date phone number and mail.

What are the technological processes?

The use of modern technologies is the key to the competitiveness of any production enterprise. The need to upgrade or replace drive systems to increase the efficiency of the enterprise and reduce the cost of equipment maintenance. It is possible to replace the drives and related units with modern equipment of greater performance. It is possible to replace the drives with more compact, but not inferior in power.

Personnel potential
composition

Personnel policy in our company is based on narrow specialization by branch of activity, i.e. a specialist with at least 15 years of work experience in the field and extensive knowledge in the industry becomes the head of a particular design bureau.

As a result, our company has 7 industry areas (metallurgical industry, rolling production, conveyor equipment, crane equipment, mining and processing equipment, chemical industry and power engineering).

We rely on their abilities and delegate responsibility to them. Both experienced and young personnel quickly get a chance at Saturn-M to discover their potential and prove themselves by working on projects, thus the company offers ideal opportunities for development and career growth.
