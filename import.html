import substitution

One of the main problems of a modern enterprise is the complexity of production processes. This is especially acute when using diverse equipment, each unit of which consists of multiple gearboxes

The gearbox is often used for decades, as a result of which the reliability required from it in operation often changes during its service time. An increase or change in the performance of the equipment that includes the gearbox does not necessarily mean that the gearbox must be replaced with a larger dimension type, because the gearbox can be equipped with additional elements (lubrication system, cooling system, monitoring system, etc.) and then successfully solve the tasks.

Saturn-M specialists will perform a detailed analysis of the specifics and operating conditions of gearboxes as part of the equipment and identify "bottlenecks" in the gearbox operation, as a result, the ordered gearbox will be engineered and the optimal solution for each specific case will be provided.
