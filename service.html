Service support

We are ready to undertake service maintenance of gearboxes and drive mechanisms of our own and third-party production, to carry out production audit and cost optimization.

Maintenance and repair

We have an opportunity to send our service team to the site to inspect the damage, as well as to assess and analyze the possible causes of damage or failure.

After assessing the external condition of the gearbox, it goes to the nearest production site of Saturn-M for a minor maintenance or a complete overhaul.

A list of defects is compiled for individual units and parts of the gearbox. We then make a cost analysis from an economic point of view, and a technical and commercial offer is sent to the customer for maintenance or repair.

After the approval of the repair or general maintenance, relevant activities are made within the agreed time frame. Replacement of faulty components, such as seals, bearings and gears is made. After assembly, the repaired device is run-in on bench equipment to ensure proper operation. Saturn-M Company positions itself as a convenient partner with all the resources to carry out cost-effective repairs.

We have the ability to perform maintenance and repair of gearboxes of our own production and third-party manufacturers.

Consultations and recommendations on operation

A large enterprise has a wide variety of production equipment and there is always a question of maintaining its proper technical condition and ensuring the continuity of production and operation of production units at maximum capacity.

The company Saturn-M, having a vast experience in production of special equipment, understands the specifics and pecularities of its work, and has the ability to share its accumulated knowledge with the service personnel at the enterprise.

We provide training, consult service personnel and give recommendations on the operation of drive systems.

Spare parts for the supplied equipment

We can produce and supply any part of the drive system, it can be gear drives for reducers, as well as individual components of the lubrication system or couplings.

We also give recommendations on how you can develop a customized strategy for stocking key wear parts for your gearboxes to ensure trouble-free machine operation.

We develop design documentation for gear drives of third-party gearboxes with the possibility of transferring them to the customer, so that there is no dependence on a specific supplier.

Technical audit

Regular check of drive components can significantly extend the drive performance and avoid unplanned downtime.

We offer a comprehensive professional audit of the entire gearbox park, the result of which is a comprehensive report with maintenance recommendations.

Collecting data and compiling a maintenance log of drive systems Analysis of downtime log data to determine the frequency of of downtime associated with faults in drive systems Visual inspection of gearboxes Communication with maintenance and service departments Analysis of possible solutions to optimize the operation of drive systems

Cost optimization

In an increasingly competitive environment, industrial holdings face the challenge of increasing the profitability and efficiency of their enterprises. However, the condition of mechanical equipment deteriorates over time, that leads to a decrease in performance and an increase in operating costs. Unplanned downtime due to the failure of drive systems and as a result of completely stops production, causing considerable loss of profits.

Based on the results of the technical audit, a deep assessment of the state of the drive systems is carried out and based on the calculation, economically measures are offered to the customer to optimize the cost of the drive system maintenance.

Measures for the replacement of drive systems that are not operating satisfactorily Measures for the improvement of the performance of drive systems that are operating satisfactorily compilation of the list of spare parts to ensure continuous operation of the equipment reduction of drive systems maintenance costs staff training

Monitoring system of equipment performance

In terms when the production basis is mechanical equipment, any failure can have dramatic consequences. One long interruption in the technological process is often the key factor, that can make an initially profitable production lose the profits. When the stakes are so high, a reliable system to protect, predict and monitor performance is an important factor.

Continuous monitoring of gearbox performance Real-time trend and status analysis Diagnostics of possible failure modes Data transfer between the concerned stakeholders Data storage Using Saturn-M technology for real-time monitoring of drive systems you get an opportunity to monitor the status of key indicators that can lead to machine failure.

Vibration level monitoring Monitoring of oil flow to the lubrication points Monitoring of bearing temperature and oil temperature Monitoring of oil level and oil presence in the gearbox Monitoring of electric motor current consumption and torque values Providing a complete overview of your production process by combining the features of Drive Monitoring Network Model and Smart Drive, we are able to provide a real-time diagnostics of all drive system key elements, improve the performance of equipment and maintain the level of performance levels.

With Saturn-M, you get a complex of hardware, software and service monitoring technologies to ensure reliable equipment maintenance, optimal condition and performance characteristics.
