Modernization

If required, Saturn-M is ready to carry out a complete replacement or modernization of gearboxes and related components of the drive system on the equipment of the enterprise in compliance with the shortest delivery and commissioning times

We are ready to replace the customer's gearbox with a more reliable and modern product, while its dimensions will be preserved or even reduced.

Taking into account the wishes of the customer, gearboxes completely identical to those operated in terms of overall and connecting dimensions can be supplied. At the same time, the internal space will be reengineered, thereby the bearing assemblies will be strengthened in the existing dimensions, reliability will be increased and the coefficient of the service factor will be strengthened.Also, the outdated gearbox dimension type can be upgraded with the usage of ground cemented gear pairs, which allows to reduce the dimensions of the gearbox by 2-3 times with a significant improvement in operational characteristic.
